# Stage 3: Building Research

Read the uploaded state and docs first.
Then do a deeper research pass on the target building.

Return:
1. a one-paragraph research summary,
2. the full replacement contents for `state/buildings.csv`,
3. the full replacement contents for `state/evidence.csv`,
4. a markdown building packet draft I can save separately,
5. a recommendation: `tour_candidate`, `backup`, or `rejected`.

Rules:

- review sites are evidence, not proof by themselves
- separate structural noise risk from location noise risk
- use `community_stability_score`, not demographic language
