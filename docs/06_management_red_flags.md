# Management and Operations Red Flags

- Vague answers on fees, parking, or lease policies
- Cannot show the exact unit or explain why not
- Pushes hard on concessions while dodging building issues
- Repeated evidence of ignored maintenance requests
- Repeated evidence of chronic noise, theft, elevator issues, or package failures
- Dirty hallways or broken amenities during the tour
- Defensive or slippery answers about after-hours issues
- No clear timeline for repairs or make-readies
- High staff turnover or obvious disorganization
- Approval timeline cannot be stated clearly
- AI-only or bot-gated leasing contact with no reliable human fallback
