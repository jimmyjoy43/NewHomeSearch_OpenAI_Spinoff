# Essential Amenities

## Unit essentials
- 1 bedroom layout or better
- In-unit washer/dryer
- Dishwasher
- Central A/C and heat
- Modern kitchen and bath finish level
- Good natural light
- Acceptable closet/storage
- Cat-friendly lease terms

## Building essentials
- Secure package handling
- Reliable maintenance
- Controlled entry or similarly strong access control
- Acceptable parking solution
- Gym
- Clean, well-maintained common areas
- Responsive leasing and management team

## Lifestyle essentials
- Quiet enough for normal daily life
- Community stability signals: low turnover, enforceable quiet hours, no hotel-like churn
- Close enough to the preferred Hollywood / West Hollywood orbit
- No confirmed severe security pattern that would change daily behavior

## Hard-stop interpretation
A building should not move forward if either:
- a required unit amenity is definitively missing, or
- building operations are clearly unacceptable, or
- approval timing makes the move schedule unrealistic.
